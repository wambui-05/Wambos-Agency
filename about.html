About Ladiva Housing Agency (L.H.A)


At Ladiva Housing Agency, we’re redefining the real estate experience by making the journey to your next home simple, secure, and satisfying. From rentals and purchases to property management, we’re here to help you find a space that truly feels like home.

Who We Are

Ladiva Housing Agency (L.H.A) is a modern real estate firm committed to delivering high-quality housing solutions tailored to the diverse needs of our clients. Since our founding in 2013, we have grown into a reputable and reliable partner for property owners and tenants across Kenya. Guided by professionalism, trust, and innovation, we connect people with homes that offer both comfort and long-term value.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide exceptional real estate services that simplify the housing process through honesty, innovation, and customer-driven solutions.

Vision: To become the leading and most dependable housing agency in Kenya — where every individual can find a home that matches their lifestyle and aspirations.

Our Services

  • Professional property management and upkeep services
  • Real estate marketing and promotional strategies
  • Personalized housing consultation and client support
  • Property investment guidance and market analysis

Why Choose Us?

At Ladiva Housing Agency, we combine market expertise with a personal touch. Our approach focuses on understanding your goals and matching them with the right property solutions.

Frequently Asked Questions


How can I book a property?
Explore our latest listings on the Home Page, select your preferred property, and click on “More”.” Our team will promptly reach out to finalize your booking and answer any related inquiries.


How do I register or access my account?
Visit the Login page to sign in or create an account. Once logged in, you can save listings, manage your preferences, and monitor your rental or purchase progress with ease.


What should I do if I forget my password?
On the Login page, click on “Forgot Password,” enter your registered email address, and follow the steps to securely reset your password and regain access to your account.


How can I contact customer support?
Our dedicated support team is always ready to assist you. Reach us through the Contact Page or email us directly at ladiva@wambo.com. We’re available Monday to Saturday, from 8:00 AM to 6:00 PM.


Where can I learn more about Ladiva Housing Agency?
Visit our About Us page to discover our vision, core values, and the exceptional services that make Ladiva a trusted name in real estate.


Need Further Assistance?
